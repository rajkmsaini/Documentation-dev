27 Description
28  Diagonal incomplete LU preconditioned BiCG solver derived from the general
29  preconditioned BiCG solver PBiCG but with the choice of preconditioner
30  pre-selected.
31 
32 Deprecated
33  This solver is present for backward-compatibility and the PBiCG solver
34  should be used instead. (deprecated Apr 2008)
