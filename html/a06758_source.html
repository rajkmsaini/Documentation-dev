27 Description
28  Cone injection.
29 
30  \li User specifies:
31  - time of start of injection
32  - injector position
33  - direction (along injection axis)
34  - parcel flow rate
35  - inner and outer half-cone angles
36 
37  \li Parcel diameters obtained by size distribution model
38 
39  \li Parcel velocity is calculated as:
40  - Constant velocity:
41  \verbatim
42  U = <specified by user>
43  \endverbatim
44 
45  - Pressure driven velocity:
46  \verbatim
47  U = sqrt(2*(Pinj - Pamb)/rho)
48  \endverbatim
49 
50  - Flow rate and discharge:
51  \verbatim
52  U = V_dot/(A*Cd)
53  \endverbatim
